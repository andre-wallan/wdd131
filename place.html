About Norway

Norway is a Scandinavian country known for its stunning natural landscapes, including fjords, mountains, and the northern lights. The country has a rich Viking heritage and is famous for its high quality of life, progressive social policies, and commitment to environmental sustainability.

With a population of about 5.4 million people, Norway is one of the world's most prosperous countries, largely due to its oil and gas resources. The capital city, Oslo, is a vibrant cultural center that perfectly blends modern architecture with historical landmarks.

Norwegian culture emphasizes outdoor activities, with concepts like "friluftsliv" (outdoor life) being central to the national identity. The country is also known for its contributions to winter sports, literature, and music.

Quick Facts
