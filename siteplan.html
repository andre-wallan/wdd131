Vocational School Portal – Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Vocational School Portal

I chose this name because it directly represents the project’s purpose: to serve as a professional online hub for vocational school students, staff, and the community. It is simple, clear, and recognizable.

Optional domain: vocationalportal.org

Site Purpose

The Vocational School Portal will provide information about the school, highlight news and events, allow students to view report cards and track attendance, and provide a contact form for inquiries. The site will also feature a references page to cite sources. It will be responsive, accessible, and include interactive JavaScript features.

Scenarios

  • “How can I check my attendance and grades online?”
  • “Where can I find the latest news and events happening at the school?”
  • “How can I contact the school administration for inquiries?”

Color Schema

The site will use two main colors:

  • Primary (#004080): Used for headings, navigation bar, and key highlights.
  • Accent (#e67e22): Used for buttons, links, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

The website will use two fonts:

  • Roboto (Sans-serif): For body text to ensure readability.
  • Merriweather (Serif): For headings to give a professional and formal look.
